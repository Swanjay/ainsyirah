# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Indonesian women aged 20-35 who want a friendly, accessible AI assistant. Many are first-time AI users, students, professionals, or content creators who prefer communicating in Bahasa Indonesia. They value warmth, simplicity, and Islamic cultural sensitivity.

## Product Purpose

A'insyirah is a free, no-signup AI chatbot that speaks Bahasa Indonesia with an Islamic-inspired personality. It exists to make AI accessible to everyday Indonesian users without the friction of registration, payment, or English-only interfaces. Success looks like a visitor who feels welcomed, understood, and empowered to ask anything.

## Brand Personality

Warm, approachable, and spiritually grounded. The brand speaks like a knowledgeable friend at a cozy cafe, not a corporate dashboard. Three words: **humble, luminous, trustworthy**. Emotional goals: calm confidence, gentle encouragement, intellectual curiosity.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with purple gradients and glassmorphism
- Corporate chatbot interfaces (cold, blue-gray, transactional)
- Overly playful "kawaii" AI assistants with cartoon mascots
- Dark-mode terminal aesthetics
- Instagram-influencer gold-on-white minimalism

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth over polish** — the interface should feel like a welcome, not a showcase
2. **Cultural resonance** — Islamic motifs and Arabic calligraphy are integral, not decorative afterthoughts
3. **Clarity first** — every element earns its place; nothing exists just to "look modern"
4. **Accessible simplicity** — works for tech-savvy users and first-timers alike
5. **Breathing space** — generous whitespace lets content and spirituality coexist

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum for all text contrast
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for users sensitive to animation
- Arabic text (Quranic verses) must use proper `dir="rtl"` and `lang="ar"`
- Font sizes must remain readable on small mobile screens (min 14px body)
- Touch targets 44x44px minimum for mobile-first Indonesian audience
